## 2. Philae Lander Components and Landing Methods
*   **Analysis of Future Solutions and Failure Causes**
    *   **Abstract**: A critical diagnostic analysis of the Philae lander’s landing failure on comet 67P/Churyumov–Gerasimenko, proposing a transition from fragile legged structures to robust, all-directional cubic architectures.
    *   **Core Logic**: Evaluating the thermal-induced degradation of the cold-gas powerplant and anchoring systems, while introducing a biomimetic adhesive and MMRTG-integrated power system to ensure mission longevity.
